# #games #meta #reviews

Blind Forest

by John Weachock in #reviews #games on Nov 3, 2019 at 9:26am

Overall: Great

10.5h played for 100% exploration and 400 deaths.

Great

  • So, so beautiful
  • Unique art style
  • No frame stutters
  • Solid pacing
  • Focused gameplay
    • Minimal filler
    • Simple mechanics
  • Great map
  • Mobility upgrades enable a lot of fun sequences
  • Exploration is very rewarding
  • Misty Woods had a super cool and unique zone mechanic
  • "Dungeon" zones were all very unique

Good

  • On-demand checkpoints take some adjusting to, but work quite well
  • Unique and charming story

Okay

  • Black Root Burrows ostensibly requires a light-producing power to explore, but actually doesn't
  • Often unclear whether an upgrade is acquirable with current upgrades

Bad

  • Platforming is slippery, making the first 1-2 hours very unforgiving. Mobility / health upgrades relieve this
  • Some mid-tier talents apply to optional abilities I didn't get until post-game
